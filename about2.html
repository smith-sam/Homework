About the

artist in the studio

I'm Samantha (Sam) Smith, based in Minnesota, and I need to create daily. I love walks in gardens, parks, and forests, and a nice cup of coffee on a sunny morning. I practice UX/UI design, Graphic Design, Drawing, Photography, Painting, Printmaking, and Sculpture.

The best part of any design or art project is seeing the process of nothing becoming something, and even better is being part of the creation of a design. I love the challenge of taking a bunch of pieces and rules for a design and bringing them together to create something visually appealing and user-friendly.

I have a background in Fine Arts with multiple gallery showings in St. Cloud and one in Alnwick, Northumberland (United Kingdom). I also have four years of in-house graphic design experience and three years of experience as a production artist. I have the drive to work in the creative field and love a challenge. Design and art are the most challenging and rewarding felids I can imagine.

UX Design Case Study

travel application design

This student project was a case study in travel apps, and while looking into how people prepare for travel, we discovered people found packing for travel anxiety-provoking. I wanted to create an app to help alleviate some packing stress. At the same time, the app would assist users in staying organized during the process. Creating a packing assistant app made to help mitigate anxiety surrounding packing and help users manage the process.

UI Design Case Study

responsive website redesign

This student project was to redesign a nonprofit organization's website, my group and I chose Lionsgate Academy. We found areas we thought we could improve the existing website.
